Meet Shamim — The Heart & Soul

Musician. Decorator. Mother to many. The visionary founder of Shamim Angel Foundation.

SHAMIM

From a melody to a movement

Born with a guitar in one hand and a heart full of compassion, Shamim always believed that art and kindness are two sides of the same soul. After witnessing the struggles of orphaned children in her community, she decided to merge her two passions — music and decoration — to build a foundation that gives second chances.

"I realized my voice could raise more than just notes. It could raise dreams. My decor skills could transform not only halls, but also futures. Every child deserves a reason to smile, and that’s why Shamim Angel Foundation exists." — Shamim, Founder

Today, Shamim performs at weddings, galas, and private celebrations while personally designing breathtaking decor themes. 100% of the profits from her artistry go directly to the foundation’s orphan care programs. She has personally mentored over 150 children and continues to be a beacon of hope.

Emotional pledge: "I will continue to sing for the ones who have no voice, and decorate life with colors of love — because every child deserves an angel."
